Health is a serious matter.

What if you didn't have free healthcare, or any healthcare at all?

They need our help.

From longstanding to emerging hazards, environmental factors are a root cause of a significant burden of death, disease and disability in developing countries. The resulting impacts are estimated to cause about 25% of death and disease globally, reaching nearly 35% in regions such as sub-Saharan Africa. This includes environmental hazards in the work, home and broader community/living environment.
